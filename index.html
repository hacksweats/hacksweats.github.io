Ways to save energy

In case you spend more than the average housesold on your energy bill, or you are just looking for ways to save money and the environment, these tips could be very helpful to you.

4-9 pm

From 4-9pm it is recommended to not use electricity. It puts a lot of stress on the grid, and it costs more as well. So, why not save some money and ensure you're using clean energy?

Reduce water heating expenses

In order to reduce water heating expenses, you must obviously use water less. An example of how to achieve this would be to shower for a smaller period of time.

Turn off the fan and lights

When you are not in the room, it is important to turn off the fan and lights in order to save on your bill. It might seem small, but after doing this for a while you will be able to clearly see the results.

Repair electrical issues in the house

Make sure to fix any electrical issues in your house. Even if it is expensive, it ensures that your equipment is more efficient and is optimizing output.

Use your window shades

An AC is one of the most expensive parts of your energy bill. So, closing your blinds in the summer to keep the sun out can be hugely infuential.

Use energy efficient LEDs

Installing energy saving lightbulbs reduces energy use by ~45%!
